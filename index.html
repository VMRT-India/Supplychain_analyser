Core Features

Everything the supply chain demands

Built around six functional pillars derived directly from production requirements.

Provenance Tracking

Register raw material batches and sub-assemblies with globally unique identifiers. Maintain full lineage from origin to final product with immutable records.

Recursive Supply Chain Modeling

Represent multi-tier supplier hierarchies as self-referencing component trees. Traverse and aggregate data across unlimited depth without limitation.

Ethical Score Computation

Compute and aggregate ethical scores across the full component tree. Scores reflect supplier compliance status, IoT conditions, and environmental metrics — recalculated on demand.

Compliance Enforcement

Block non-compliant batch insertions at the database level. Propagate violations across entire dependency trees and restore compliant suppliers automatically.

IoT Data Integration

Ingest real-time sensor readings per batch. Validate temperature and humidity against thresholds, flag violations, and queue score deductions as background tasks.

Consumer Transparency & Audit Logging

Expose full product provenance, compliance status, and event history to consumers via QR code. All system events are recorded in an append-only, categorised audit log.

User Roles

Four roles. Controlled access.

Every role interacts through a role-gated dashboard. Permissions are enforced strictly at the backend level.

Admin

Administrator

Full system access. Approves or flags suppliers, triggers score recalculations, refreshes materialized views, and reviews all audit logs. Requires MFA.

Supplier

Supplier

Registers product batches, submits IoT sensor data, and records ethical metrics such as carbon footprint and worker safety scores for their components.

Auditor

Auditor

Reviews supplier compliance status, issues ethical certificates, and queries the audit log. Elevated access requires MFA verification at login.

Consumer

Consumer

Read-only access to product history and provenance data. Can scan QR codes to retrieve origin, ethical score, compliance status, and audit events.
